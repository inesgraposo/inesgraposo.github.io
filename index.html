Welcome to my website! I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at Bocconi University.

I am an applied macroeconomist. My current research focuses on the impact of heterogeneous expectations in the labor market. I aim to understand how workers' and firms' expectations influence decision-making, aggregate outcomes, and optimal policy design.

This website is updated once in a blue moon (rather once a year). If you wish to know more about what I am doing, the best way is to get in touch.
